Common Questions

Before We Connect

What does a first conversation look like.

We listen. You share where your organization is, what is working, and what feels unsustainable. We ask questions to understand the full picture. If there is a fit, we outline possible paths forward. If not, we say so honestly.

Do you work with organizations outside of North Carolina.

Yes. While LUR Growth is based in Winston-Salem, we work with nonprofits nationally. Most engagements combine virtual sessions with on-site work as needed.

How long does a typical engagement last.

Most structured engagements run 90 days. The Executive Advisory Retainer is ongoing. We scope every engagement to fit the organization's actual needs, not a predetermined template.

What size organizations do you work with.

We work with organizations of varying size, from small community-based nonprofits to larger intermediaries and foundation-supported initiatives. The common thread is a commitment to building infrastructure that lasts.
