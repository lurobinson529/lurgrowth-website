Services

Six engagement models designed to meet nonprofits wherever they are.

We help nonprofits stop carrying strategy on people and start embedding it into systems, so shifts in funding, policy, and leadership do not destabilize the organization.

Service 01

Strategic Impact Execution

Phase 1, 90-Day Execution Architecture

This is where strategy becomes operational. In 90 days, we translate your organization's strategic goals into clear execution systems with defined ownership, measurable milestones, and sustainable workflows.

The result is an architecture that your team can run without depending on a single person to hold it together. Goals become trackable. Responsibilities become visible. Progress becomes measurable.

What This Includes

  • Organizational assessment and strategic alignment review
  • Execution architecture design with clear milestones
  • Workflow mapping and ownership assignment
  • Progress tracking systems and accountability structures
  • 90-day implementation roadmap

Service 02

Strategic Impact Execution

Phase 2, Systems Embedding

Building on the architecture from Phase 1, this engagement embeds strategy into the daily operations of your organization. The goal is simple: when people leave, transition, or shift roles, the work continues without disruption.

We design knowledge transfer protocols, operational documentation, and decision-making frameworks that live inside the organization, not inside any one person.

What This Includes

  • Systems integration and operational embedding
  • Knowledge transfer protocols and documentation
  • Decision-making frameworks and escalation paths
  • Staff training and capacity building
  • Sustainability assessment and handoff planning

Service 03

Transitional Bridge Engagement

90 Days

Leadership transitions, funding shifts, and structural changes can destabilize even strong organizations. This engagement provides steady, strategic support while your organization navigates the in-between.

We hold the strategic center so the team can focus on the transition itself. The organization stays grounded while decisions get made, roles get clarified, and operations continue moving forward.

Best Suited For

  • Executive director transitions
  • Board restructuring or governance shifts
  • Major funder changes or grant cycle disruptions
  • Organizational mergers or program consolidation
  • Post-crisis stabilization periods

Service 04

Crisis Reset

90 Days

For organizations in acute distress, this engagement provides immediate assessment, stabilization, and a clear path forward. We do not come in with assumptions. We assess what is actually happening, identify the most urgent structural gaps, and rebuild the operational foundation.

The goal is to move leadership from reactive survival to deliberate, grounded progress within 90 days.

What This Addresses

  • Financial instability or cash flow crises
  • Board dysfunction or governance breakdown
  • Staff turnover and operational collapse
  • Loss of major funding or program discontinuation
  • Reputational challenges requiring structural response

Service 05

Regenerative Nonprofit Leadership

Executive Advisory Retainer

This is a sustained advisory partnership for executive directors and senior leaders who want ongoing strategic counsel. Not a coach. Not a consultant who shows up once. A trusted advisor who understands your organization deeply and helps you navigate decisions with clarity.

The retainer model allows for consistent, responsive support that adapts to the rhythm of your leadership, whether you are preparing for a board meeting, navigating a funding conversation, or making a difficult personnel decision.

What This Includes

  • Regular strategic advisory sessions
  • Board preparation and governance guidance
  • Organizational health monitoring
  • Decision-making support for complex situations
  • Priority access for time-sensitive matters

Service 06

Capacity Advisory

Systems Design and Implementation

We design systems that reduce manual staff burden, create operational clarity, and free leadership to focus on mission. The outcome is measurable: time saved, money protected, focus restored.

This is not about adopting new platforms for the sake of modernization. It is about identifying where your team is spending time on tasks that could be systematized, and building the infrastructure to reclaim that capacity for mission-critical work.

Outcomes

  • Reduced manual administrative burden
  • Automated reporting and data visibility
  • Streamlined workflows and process documentation
  • Operational clarity across teams and departments
  • Leadership time redirected to strategic priorities
