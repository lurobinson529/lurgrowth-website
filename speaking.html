Latoya Robinson Speaking

Latoya brings over a decade of nonprofit leadership experience to stages across the sector. She speaks to Executive Directors, board members, and emerging leaders on capacity building, strategic fundraising, and sustainable nonprofit infrastructure.

Her sessions are grounded in real operational experience, not theory. Audiences leave with clarity about what needs to change, and a practical understanding of how to begin.
