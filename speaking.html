Speaking and Thought Leadership

20 years in the nonprofit sector. 15+ as an Executive Director. Real operational experience brought to stages across the sector.

Latoya brings 20 years in the nonprofit sector and 15+ years as an Executive Director to stages across the sector. She speaks to Executive Directors, board members, and emerging leaders on capacity building, agile nonprofit infrastructure, leadership sustainability, and the Impact Flow framework.

Her sessions are grounded in real operational experience, not theory. Audiences leave with clarity about what needs to change, and a practical understanding of how to begin.
