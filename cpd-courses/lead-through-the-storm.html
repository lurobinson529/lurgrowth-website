About the course

A five-module CPD-accredited course for nonprofit leaders navigating burnout, depletion, and leadership fatigue. Lead Through the Storm™ is the inner foundation course that helps leaders recognize their storm, reclaim their energy, reconnect with purpose, and build systems that sustain their calm long term.

This course goes beyond surface-level self-care advice. It provides a structured, regenerative framework that equips leaders with the tools and practices they need to move from survival mode to sustained, purpose-driven leadership.

What you will build

  • Nonprofit Storm Scale Self-Assessment
  • Emotional Regulation Practice Plan
  • Energy Alignment Grid
  • 90-Day Storm Plan
  • Calm Integration Plan
  • My Purpose Alignment Map
