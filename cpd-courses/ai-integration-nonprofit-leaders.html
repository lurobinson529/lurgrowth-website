About the course

A six-module CPD-accredited course designed to help nonprofit leaders integrate AI tools into real organizational workflows. Built on the Nonprofit Capacity Architecture framework and applicable whether you are a first-time AI user or building on existing knowledge.

By the end of this course, participants will have completed practical deliverables they can implement immediately, including a Current State Tool Map, AI Readiness Assessment Scorecard, Workflow Integration Map, AI Acceptable Use Policy Draft, 90-Day AI Integration Roadmap, and AI Impact Metrics Framework with a Stakeholder Communication Draft.
