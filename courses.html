About CPD Accreditation

Continuing Professional Development (CPD) accreditation is an internationally recognized standard for structured professional learning. All LUR Growth courses have been independently reviewed and accredited by the CPD Certification Service, confirming they meet rigorous standards for educational quality and professional relevance.

lurgrowth.com is the registered CPD provider. Upon completion of each course, participants receive a verifiable CPD Certificate of Completion that can be used for professional development records, funder reporting, and organizational capacity documentation.

What CPD Accreditation Means

  • ✓ Independent quality review and accreditation
  • ✓ Internationally recognized certification
  • ✓ Verifiable Certificate of Completion
  • ✓ Structured learning with measurable outcomes
  • ✓ Suitable for professional development records
